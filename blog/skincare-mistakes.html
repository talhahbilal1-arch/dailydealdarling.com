Common Skincare Mistakes to Stop Making

Mistake #1: Over-exfoliating
Scrubbing daily destroys your skin barrier. Chemical exfoliants 2-3x per week max are enough. More isn't better.
Mistake #2: Skipping SPF
Sun damage causes more aging than almost anything else. Daily sunscreen is non-negotiable, even if you're inside most of the day.
Mistake #3: Hot water and harsh cleansers
Hot water strips oils and irritates skin. Lukewarm water and gentle cleansers preserve your skin barrier.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I'm over-exfoliating?

Signs: redness, irritation, excessive dryness or oiliness, breakouts, sensitivity. If your skin is reactive, stop exfoliating and let your barrier heal.

Do I need sunscreen if I work from home?

Yes - UVA rays come through windows. If you're near windows during the day, you need protection. Plus consistency builds the habit.

Should my cleanser make my face feel tight?

No! That tight feeling means stripped skin. A good cleanser removes dirt without removing all your natural oils.

How long until I see results from fixing mistakes?

About 4-6 weeks for your skin to adjust and show improvement. Be patient and consistent.

The Bottom Line

Sometimes better skincare means doing less - gentler products, less scrubbing, more protecting. These three products fixed the mistakes I didn't know I was making and finally gave my skin a chance to heal.
