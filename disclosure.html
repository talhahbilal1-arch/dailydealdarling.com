Last Updated: December 31, 2024

Quick Summary: Daily Deal Darling is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. When you click links on this site and make a purchase, I may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This helps support the site and allows me to continue creating free content.

What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are special URLs that track when a visitor clicks through and makes a purchase. When you buy something through an affiliate link on this site, I receive a small percentage of the sale. This doesn't cost you anything extra—you pay the same price you would if you went directly to the retailer.

How I Use Affiliate Links

On Daily Deal Darling, I include affiliate links in:

  • Product recommendations within blog posts
  • Product cards with "Check Price on Amazon" buttons
  • The "Current Favorites" section on the homepage

All affiliate links are marked with "#ad" near the product information.
